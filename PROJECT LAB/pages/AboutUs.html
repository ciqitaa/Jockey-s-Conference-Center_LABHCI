OVERVIEW

JCC was established in 1974 and known as a famous conference center in Jakarta, Indonesia. JCC has held many important national and international conference, exhibition, fair, indoor sports, and musical events were held at JCC, including 10th Non-Aligned Movement Conference and Asia-African Conference. It is a very venue and conference place throughout the year. JCC is more than a conference center.

Jockey’s Conference Center has 13 conference meeting venue with different size of venue, including the theater-style venue (Plenary Hall), 2 exhibition halls, an Assembly Hall, VIP room. JCC gives everything to support your conference.
