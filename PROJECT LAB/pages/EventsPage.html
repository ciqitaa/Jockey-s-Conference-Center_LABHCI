ASIA PACIFIC COATING SHOW

14 - 16 October 2023
Exhibition Hall B

The Asia Pacific Coating Show is a leading trade event in the coatings and paint industry, specifically catering to the Asia Pacific region. It attracts manufacturers, suppliers, and professionals from various sectors within the industry. The show provides a platform for exhibitors to showcase their latest products, technologies, and services. It also serves as a networking hub, allowing attendees to connect with potential business partners, learn about emerging trends, and gain insights from industry experts. With its comprehensive exhibition, seminars, and conferences, the Asia Pacific Coating Show plays a crucial role in promoting innovation, collaboration, and growth in the coatings and paint sector.

INDONESIA MOTORCYCLE SHOW

2 - 6 November 2023
Exhibition

The Indonesia Motorcycle Show is a significant exhibition dedicated to the motorcycle industry in Indonesia. It serves as a platform for motorcycle manufacturers, distributors, and enthusiasts to showcase their latest models, accessories, and technologies. The show attracts a wide range of attendees, including industry professionals, motorcycle enthusiasts, and potential buyers. It offers opportunities for networking, business collaborations, and gaining insights into the latest trends and innovations in the motorcycle industry. The Indonesia Motorcycle Show plays a vital role in promoting the growth of the motorcycle sector in Indonesia, while also providing an engaging and informative experience for visitors.

JAKARTA X BEAUTY 2023

12 - 14 December 2023
Exhibition Hall A+B

Jakarta X Beauty is a prominent beauty trade exhibition held in JCC. It is a platform for industry professionals, beauty brands, and enthusiasts to showcase and discover the new brands, trends, and technologies. This event attract a diverse attendees, including the beauty industry leaders, retailers, salon owners, and the one and only beauty enthusiasts. This event also provides opportunities for networking, business collaboration, and gaining insights about beauty industry. With the exhibitions, educational sessions, and live demonstrations, this event also aims to promote growth, innovation, and knowledge about beauty and cosmetics sector in Jakarta.
