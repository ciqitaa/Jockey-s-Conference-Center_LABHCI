About JCC

The Jockey’s Conference Center (JCC) is a prestigious venue located in Jakarta, Indonesia. With over 40,000 square meters of exhibition space, it serves as a hub for hosting various events including conferences, exhibitions, trade shows, and concerts. Situated in the heart of Jakarta's business district, the JCC features state-of-the-art facilities, advanced audio-visual equipment, and high-speed internet connectivity. Its strategic location provides easy access to nearby accommodations, shopping centers, and entertainment venues. The JCC is a preferred choice for both national and international event organizers, thanks to its modern infrastructure, central location, and ability to cater to events of different scales.
